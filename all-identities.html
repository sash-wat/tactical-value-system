The 6 Stable Tactical Archetypes

The TVMS taxonomy scores team profiles using standard scaler coefficients and GMM cluster centroids pooled across the historical 2020–2023 cohorts. Teams are classified under the archetype they align closest with in 9-dimensional space, with a calibrated threshold highlighting hybrid systems.

High-Efficiency Passing

Primary Metric: Possession Quality (%)

Teams that maintain high pass completion rates and dominate games through patient, structured possession build-up.

High-Line Pressing

Primary Metric: Opponent Directness

Defensive systems that restrict short build-ups and force direct opponent clearances by deploying aggressive high blocks.

High-Disruption Defense

Primary Metric: Defensive Disruption (g+)

Intense midfield defensive setups that disrupt opposition transition actions and force high turnover volumes.

Progressive Dribbling

Primary Metric: Dribbling Impact (g+)

Dynamic transitional units relying on elite ball carriers to bypass defenders and break opposition lines.

Balanced Systems

Primary Metric: None (Tactical Equilibrium)

Systems that maintain statistical balance across all phases of play with no singular dominant stylistic deviation.

Direct Attacking

Primary Metric: Attacking Directness

High-tempo vertical setups designed to bypass midfield play and quickly launch actions into open space behind defenses.
